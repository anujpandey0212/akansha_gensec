Main Points

Women-centric

Regarding Women centric issues I would like to establish a student driven arm of the Women’s Cell which would act in conjunction with them to:

Bring Workshops, Programs and Initiatives under the regular initiatives of the student community to enhance participation and feedback from the public.

Properly maintain the Women’s Cell webpage and periodically update it’s objectives to make it the go-to spot for addressing any issues and for new learning opportunities.

Finally Improving the digital presence of the Cell with the hope for External Collaboration.

An effort will be made towards the Health and Wellbeing of the women in the institute wherein Sanitary product dispensers shall be installed in the washrooms in the Academic Blocks as well as Women’s Hostels.

Parallelly, Disposal systems for them will also be established.

Health and Well-Being

For improving the Health and Wellbeing of the students as well as some Quality-of-Life improvements I will strive for the:

Facilitation of a small Pharmacy under the hostels for quick access to medical supplies. Provision of First Aid facilities near the Sports complex, Gym and Labs.

Regular restocking of the supplies kept in the First Aid boxes deployed in the hostels. Dedicated space for Councils in the upcoming SAC room.

Dedicated storage space for clubs with expensive or fragile equipment which will still be easily accessible to club members in the upcoming SAC room.

An Award room for displaying the awards won by students and teams in external competitions in the hostels.

Academics

For the streamlining of UG research opportunities I will work towards:

Creation of more undergraduate opportunities via collaboration with faculties, research labs, competitions etc. (By setting up periodic meets through career cell, streamlined platform)

Improving and maintaining access to scientific journals with IITH Email along with organizing the services which are already provided so they can be utilized properly.

Continuing the other services at least for Club access/Inter IIT/ Research groups which would obviously need them.

Infrastructure

Upgrading the Gymkhana printer for giving the students access to high quality colour printouts of larger size as well as streamlining the process for getting printouts from the Gymkhana printer.

Push for the testing and maintenance of Radiant cooling before the end of Feb to pre-emptively take care of any issues which would halt cooling in the summer season.

Push for the provision of at least two Industrial grade water filters under each block to provide cool and clean water at a moment’s notice

Setting up Wi-Fi boosters wherever internet access is necessary but Wi-Fi signal is poor such as the canteen, Acad C7 etc.

Streamlining Processes

Establishing a centralized calendar which will have information regarding academic deadlines, student centric events, external examination information for both placements and further studies, to allow better planning and distribution on events and prevent clashes and dry spells.

Coming up with an Organized complaint system for the students. Being able to view stats regarding your complaint such as expected time of resolution, complaint status as well as community statistics will bring more transparency and confidence into the system and its users.

Logs for the complete history of decisions made and processes involved will be maintained and made public to keep the public much more informed and involved in the process. This will include events such as:

Opening and closing of forms and actual meeting times.

Information regarding the actual parties involved in the decision making, as well as those who signed off onto it including decision reviewers and approvers.

Work towards greater inclusivity of student driven organization which are not recognized as clubs into the affairs of student bodies. This will bring more vibrancy and diversity to club cultures and may drive innovation. (Popularizing the Informal Club Association)

Ensure consistent laundry services near hostel blocks.

Efforts for more productive and clear sharing of information on mail as well as online forums will be taken.

Transport

Arrangement of busses for airport and railway station during peak travel times; i.e. before holidays.

Reinitiating the bus services to Lingampally, Sangareddy and Miyapur.

Provision of a secure Cab service for travel of Women to Airport and Railway station at night.

Procure vendors for pushing the purchase of cycles of at low prices for all students to encourage the use of them and reduce burden on existing transport infrastructure.
